Recent projects

  • Snake

    An homage to the old Nokia cellphone game that became popular in the 90s. Maneuver the snake and collect points by eating fruits. The snakes size and speed increases by every consumed fruit and the game ends when you collide with either a wall or the snakes own body.


  • Quizfajten

    A quiz game. Answer questions and get as many correct answers as possible to win. You can choose from four different categories and play against different people online.


  • Gym login

    A simple gym management application that prompts the customer to login either by name or social security number and then checks if the membership is valid. It also stores the dates of the logins to keep track of when and how often you work out.
